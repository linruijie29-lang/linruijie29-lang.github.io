About

Finance & Tech,
Synthesized

Richard is a college freshman at NYU Stern pursuing the Business, Technology & Entrepreneurship (BTE) program — one of the most selective tracks at Stern, designed for students determined to shape the future of both industries.

His work spans early-stage startup sprints, hackathon innovation challenges, and venture capital case competitions. Each experience sharpens the same conviction: the most compelling careers live where rigorous financial thinking and technological fluency reinforce each other.

Richard aspires to join the technology division of a leading financial institution, building tools and strategies that move capital smarter, faster, and more responsibly.
